2. **E3 — Creature death visibility loss:** 1-tick delay acceptable, test required

4. **E11 — ExploredTileCache consistency:** Cache on `onAdd`, not `onRemove`
5. **E14 — Tile access pattern:** Breaking change from index-based to coordinate-based

### Performance Assessment
