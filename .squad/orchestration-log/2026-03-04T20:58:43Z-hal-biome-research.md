## Key Findings

- **Core Verb Insight:** Biome sim verb is "influence" (or "nudge"), NOT "build" or "gather"
- **Genre Pillars:** Equilinox, Ecosystem, Terra Nil, Niche, SimLife demonstrate pattern of emergent complexity from simple rules
- **Multiplayer Gold Standard:** Eco (team-based ecosystem governance)
- **Player Control:** Best sims center on "observational toys with levers"—small decisions, large cascading effects
- **Primal Grid Readiness:** ~60% architecturally ready (has grid, creature AI/FSM, ecosystem sim, multiplayer sync). Missing: meaningful player influence over biome development, visible emergent outcomes, shared ecosystem tension.
